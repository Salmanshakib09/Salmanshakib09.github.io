Research Project

Thermal Care of Newborns in Bangladesh

This undergraduate research project investigated socio-economic and demographic factors associated with thermal care practices for newborns in Bangladesh using the Bangladesh Demographic and Health Survey 2017–18. The project involved public health data analysis, statistical modeling, and interpretation of survey-based findings.

Methods used: descriptive statistics, chi-square tests, regression analysis, and interpretation of demographic and socio-economic factors.
