Project: Thermal Care of Newborns in Bangladesh

This project investigated socio-economic and demographic factors associated with thermal care practices for newborns in Bangladesh using BDHS 2017–18 data.

Methods included descriptive statistics, chi-square tests, regression analysis, and interpretation of survey-based public health findings.

Project: Antenatal Care Coverage in Bangladesh

This project examined women’s decision-making autonomy and its relationship with complete antenatal care coverage using BDHS 2017–18 data.

The project focused on maternal health, survey data analysis, demographic factors, and applied statistical methods for public health research.
